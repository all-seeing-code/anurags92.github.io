Hi ! I am Anurag, currently in my senior year of undergraduate degree. I am interested in Maths, Programming and Literature. This little blog is primarily for personal use and is meant to keep track of my thoughts and writings.


I grew up in New Delhi, India, which is a really big and happening city and there's a really good chance you'll easily get lost in the hustle.


I spent Spring'15 in Lausanne, Switzerland and worked as software developer intern at COSMO, EPFL which is a really nice laboratory working on developing tools and methods for molecular simulation on computer. I also had opportunity to work with Prof. János Pach while in Switzerland. Truly a remarkable experience.
